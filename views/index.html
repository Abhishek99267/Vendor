About VendorLink

VendorLink is a secure and scalable Vendor Management System developed using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, Express.js and MongoDB. The platform helps businesses organize supplier information, monitor purchase orders, evaluate vendor performance and improve communication.

👤

Vendor Management

Store and manage complete vendor information including company details, GST number, address, contact information and category.

📦

Purchase Orders

Create, update and monitor purchase orders, delivery status, payment status and inventory supplies from one place.

⭐

Performance Evaluation

Evaluate vendors using quality, delivery, communication and cost ratings to identify the best suppliers.

💬

Communication

Send messages to suppliers, maintain communication history and improve collaboration with vendors.
